XR / VR Healing Landscape Ongoing

CureLandscape VR

An ongoing VR experiment exploring homesickness, emotional restoration, and healing landscape design for international students.

Project Overview

CureLandscape VR is an ongoing research-led virtual reality experiment focused on homesickness and emotional wellbeing among international students. The project explores how restorative landscape design, memory, and immersive atmosphere can support emotional comfort inside virtual space.

Research Direction

The work sits between XR, HCI, environmental psychology, and healing landscape studies. It asks how virtual environments can be designed not only as simulations, but as emotionally supportive spaces that reconnect users with familiarity, belonging, and sensory calm.

Current Status

The project is currently in progress. The present stage focuses on defining the visual language, emotional framing, and interaction structure of the healing landscape prototype.
